Sisig Tokwa

Description

Today we are veganizing the famous Kapampangan dish, sisig! Tofu Sisig is made with crispy tofu mixed with spicy peppers and other aromatics and is packed with so much flavor! Crispy tofu is used in place of meat but don't let that fool you; it's crispy, delicious, and easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • Firm Tofu: 1 block, pressed and cubed
  • Pork Belly: 1/2 pound, minced
  • Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Red Chili Peppers: 2-3 pieces, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • Calamansi or Lime: 2 pieces, juiced
  • Soy Sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Mayonnaise: 3 tablespoons
  • Salt and Pepper: to taste
  • Green Onions: for garnish, chopped

Steps:

  1. Press and cube the firm tofu.
  2. In a pan, fry the tofu cubes until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté minced pork until cooked and slightly crispy.
  4. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the pork. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  5. Add red chili peppers and continue cooking for a few minutes.
  6. Combine the fried tofu with the pork mixture in the pan.
  7. Pour soy sauce and calamansi/lime juice over the mixture. Mix well.
  8. Add mayonnaise and stir until all ingredients are coated.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Garnish with chopped green onions.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your Sisig Tokwa!
