Pinoy Spaghetti

Description

Pinoy Spaghetti is a Filipino-style spaghetti that has a distinct sweet and savory flavor. It is known for its vibrant red sauce and the inclusion of hotdogs and ground meat

Pinoy Spaghetti is often enjoyed during celebrations and gatherings, and its unique sweet and savory taste is a favorite among Filipinos. Adjust the sweetness and other seasonings to suit your personal preference.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles: 400g (about 14 ounces)
  • Ground Pork or Beef: 250g (about 8-10 ounces)
  • Hotdogs: 4-6 pieces, sliced diagonally
  • Tomato Sauce: 1 can (about 400g)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Banana Ketchup: 1/2 to 3/4 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Onion: 1 medium-sized, finely chopped
  • Red Bell Pepper: 1 piece, diced (optional)
  • Cheese: 1/2 cup, grated (typically processed cheese, like Eden or Quickmelt)
  • Evaporated Milk: 1/2 cup
  • Sugar: 1-2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and Pepper: to taste
  • Cooking Oil: for sautéing

Steps

  1. Boiling the Spaghetti:
    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
    • Add salt and a bit of oil to the boiling water.
    • Cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Sautéing Aromatics:
    • In a separate pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat.
    • Sauté minced garlic until golden brown.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until softened.
  3. Cooking Meat:
    • Add the ground pork or beef to the sautéed garlic and onions.
    • Cook until the meat is browned and cooked through.
  4. Adding Hotdogs and Red Bell Pepper:
    • Incorporate sliced hotdogs and diced red bell pepper into the meat mixture.
    • Cook for a few more minutes until the hotdogs are slightly browned.
  5. Creating the Sauce:
    • Stir in the grated cheese and evaporated milk.
    • Season with sugar, salt, and pepper to achieve the desired sweet and savory balance.
    • Continue to simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Assembling:
    • Combine the cooked spaghetti noodles with the prepared sauce.
    • Toss until the noodles are well-coated with the sauce.
  7. Serving:
    • Serve the Pinoy Spaghetti hot, garnished with additional grated cheese if desired.
