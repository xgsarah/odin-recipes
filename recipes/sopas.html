Sopas

Description

"Sopas" is a Filipino term for a type of soup that is often associated with comfort food. It is a creamy and hearty chicken soup with macaroni pasta, vegetables, and sometimes diced ham. Sopas is a popular dish in the Philippines, especially during cold weather or when someone is feeling under the weather. It is often served for breakfast, merienda (snack time), or as a light meal.

Sopas is often garnished with chopped scallions or fried garlic before serving. It's a comforting and filling soup enjoyed by many in Filipino households.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup ham, diced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the elbow macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced carrots and cook until slightly softened.
  4. Sprinkle flour over the vegetables, stirring constantly to make a roux.
  5. Gradually pour in the chicken broth while stirring to avoid lumps. Bring to a boil.
  6. Add the shredded chicken and diced ham. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in the shredded cabbage and cooked macaroni. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Pour in the evaporated milk and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  9. Adjust seasoning if necessary, and serve hot.
